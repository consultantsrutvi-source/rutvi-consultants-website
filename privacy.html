Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and handle your details with care.

When you contact Rutvi Consultants by phone, WhatsApp or email, we may collect basic information such as your name, contact number, email address and details about your institute or teaching requirement.

We use this information only to:

  • Respond to your enquiry
  • Share relevant information about faculty or vacancies
  • Coordinate communication between institutes and lecturers

We do not sell your personal information to unrelated third parties for marketing. Information is shared only with the parties directly involved in the consultancy process, where it is relevant and handled with reasonable care.

If you would like us to update or delete your contact details from our records, please email us at Consultantsrutvi@gmail.com.
