About Rutvi Consultants

Rutvi Consultants is a professional educational recruitment and consultancy firm dedicated to strengthening the education system by bridging the gap between institutions and talented educators.

We specialize in the recruitment of qualified and experienced faculty for JEE, NEET, KCT, and CBSE board coaching and institutions across India. With a strong network of lecturers and academic professionals, we ensure that every institution gets the right educator for the right role.

Our consultancy follows a transparent, ethical, and result-oriented recruitment process. We focus on long-term relationships and aim to support both institutions and educators throughout the hiring journey.

Our Mission

Our mission is to improve the quality of education in India by providing highly skilled, trained, and dedicated teaching professionals to educational institutions and coaching centers nationwide.

Our Vision

To become a trusted and leading educational recruitment consultancy in India, known for quality, integrity, and excellence in academic staffing.

Our Team

A small, focused team combining academic understanding and operational experience.

Academic Coordination

Recruitment & Screening

Manages faculty sourcing, screening, basic verification and shortlisting for different subjects and boards.

Institute & Educator Support

Takes care of communication, follow-ups and coordination so that both institutes and lecturers have a smooth experience.

We keep our structure lean and efficient so that we can respond faster and stay closely involved with every requirement.
