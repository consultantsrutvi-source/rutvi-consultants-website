Our Charges – For Lecturers

We keep our charges simple and transparent. We do not charge anything for registration or just sharing openings. Charges apply only after you successfully join an institute through Rutvi Consultants.

  • Who pays? Only lecturers pay consultancy charges. Institutes are not charged.
  • When? Charges are applicable only after your joining is confirmed.
  • Onboarding fee: A minimal amount of ₹499 is collected at the time of joining. This is used towards documentation, bill generation and welcome formalities.
  • Total consultancy fee: Our consultancy fee remains 50% of your first month’s salary (the ₹499 is counted as part of this total).
  • Payment structure (EMIs):
    • Step 1 – ₹499 onboarding fee at the time of joining.
    • Step 2 – The remaining amount (total 50% minus ₹499) is collected later from your first month salary in 2 easy EMIs.

Example: If your first month’s salary is ₹40,000, total consultancy fee is ₹20,000. ₹499 is collected at joining, and the remaining ₹19,501 is taken later in 2 EMIs as discussed with you before joining.

All terms are clearly explained to you in advance. There are no hidden charges, and all payments are supported by proper billing.
