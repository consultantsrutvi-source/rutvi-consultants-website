For Institutes – Faculty Support

If you run or manage a college, school or coaching institute, we help you recruit suitable faculty for JEE, NEET, KCET and CBSE boards.

Share Your Requirements

Tell us the subjects, board or exam (JEE/NEET/KCET/CBSE), batch strength, timings and any specific expectations you have from faculty.

Faculty Suggestions

Based on your requirements, we suggest lecturers who may be a good fit and are interested in working with institutions like yours.

Coordination & Connection

With consent, we connect you with shortlisted lecturers for further discussion, demo lectures or interviews.

Our Faculty Screening Process

  • Verification of prior subject knowledge and teaching experience (based on CV / discussion).
  • Demo lecture evaluation where required by the institution.
  • Basic salary expectation discussion with the lecturer.
  • Previous institute / experience confirmation where reasonably possible.
  • Only suitable and interested faculty profiles are forwarded to your institution.

This structured screening helps increase reliability and saves valuable time for colleges and coaching institutes during the hiring process.

Why Choose Us – For Institutes

  • Verified and experienced lecturers recommended for your requirements.
  • Fast and reliable hiring process with focused coordination.
  • Transparent and simple communication at every step.
  • Strong academic network built with multiple educators.
  • Dedicated support for institutions from requirement to joining stage.

Charges for Institutes

Currently, we do not charge any consultancy fees from institutes.
Our consultancy charges are taken only from lecturers after successful joining, as per the terms explained to them.
