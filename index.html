About Rutvi Consultants

Rutvi Consultants is an education consultancy that connects NEET & JEE coaching institutes with suitable teaching faculty.

We understand that the quality of faculty makes a big difference in the performance of students preparing for competitive exams like NEET and JEE. Our role is to act as a bridge between institutes that need dependable subject teachers and lecturers who are looking for the right workplace.

Our work is based on professionalism, transparency and simple communication. We focus on long-term relationships where both institutes and lecturers feel supported.

How We Work

Our process is kept simple so institutes and lecturers can focus on teaching.

  1. Coaching institute shares its faculty requirement with us.
  2. We understand subject, timings, batch strength and other basic details.
  3. We identify lecturers who may be suitable and interested.
  4. With consent, we help both sides connect for further discussion.
  5. If both sides agree, they directly finalise their terms and conditions.

Note: Rutvi Consultants only assists in connecting institutes and lecturers. Final decisions, salary, and other terms are decided directly by them.
